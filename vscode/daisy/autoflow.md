# Autoflow and Daisy

## Integration with client's Google Drive

### Work for Us

Daisy can import designs from clients' Google Drive.
Client authorizes Daisy to access their Google Drive (read).
Client can pick any file they have on their Google Drive or upload local file (existing functionality).

### Work for Autoflow

Client authorizes Autoflow to access their Google Drive (write).
Or Daisy on behalf of client authorizes Autoflow to their Google Drive (only possible if we host both).
Autoflow connects to Google Drive and can save files there.
Terminating Autoflow app returns to Daisy app.
Client can pick any file they have on their Google Drive or upload local file (existing functionality).

## Ability to open and save files from Google Drive

Requires above.

## Ability to export and import local files

When using Autoflow, allow to save design locally (to disk).
Allow to load designs from disk.
Now group of clients to share their designs via email or generally outside of Google Drive.
